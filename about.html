About Us

We offer comprehensive training in Manual and Automation Testing, combining theory with real-time practice. Our course covers various testing types, using real-world applications for functionality, security, and performance testing. Students work with industry-standard tools and improve skills in communication and presentation. We conduct mock interviews to simulate company environments, ensuring students are well-prepared. We also assist with resume building, Naukri profile creation, and provide HR contacts, guiding through the entire hiring process. With placement guarantee, our course prepares you for the growing demand in software testing, essential in today’s AI and automation-driven world.

Mission

  • To bridge the gap between candidates and the corporate / IT sector by creating skilled resources who can build their career in the industry.
  • To become a leading and well-known institute for creating skilled professionals which sets a benchmark for others to follow.

Vision

  • To provide candidates with technical knowledge that is at-par with the IT industry, but also in a pocket-friendly way so that everyone can benefit from our courses.
  • To provide training to candidates in various technologies by experts who have first-hand industry knowledge so that candidates can start their career and be in sync with the industry.
  • To bridge the expectation gap between the corporate sector and candidates.
  • To provide corporate training to the candidates as per their requirements.

Why Tech Hub?

  • We strive to teach the latest and most advanced technologies to our candidates so that they are not left behind while the industry is making progress.
  • Our faculty consists of experienced, real-time working IT professionals which means that candidates get trained with the latest knowledge base.
  • We provide placements to candidates who successfully complete our training courses and after cracking third-party external expert interviews.
  • We have kept our training programs very cost-effective so as to ensure that everyone can take benefit of our courses.
  • Candidates can attend the classes as many times as they want till they get placed. This ensures that they can keep themselves updated for interviews, clear their doubts on a topic, and also cover up on any classes they may have missed.
  • We believe that candidates will have a lot of confidence when they know that we are with them all the way in making their career.
  • During the course, candidates get to face mock interviews by industry experts. This gives them the benefit of facing real-life interviews in the course itself.
  • We provide maximum support to candidates even after they get placed.
  • During their course, candidates get to work on live projects, giving them exposure to scenarios from the industry.
  • We have a great lab facility and infrastructure to complement our excellent faculty.
  • On weekends, we arrange guest seminars by industry experts.
